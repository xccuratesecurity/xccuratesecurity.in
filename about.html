About Us



Who We Are

Xccurate Security is a cyber security based company. Xccurate Security is embracing digitization that are prone to IT risks. We all know that emerging technologies are putting cybersecurity at risk. Even sometimes the new advancements in defensive strategies for professional securities are failing at some point. But combining the strength of artificial intelligence (AI) algorithms with cybersecurity, professional securities have additional resources to defend the vulnerable network and data from cyber attackers. After combining these technologies and applying them brings instant insights, which results in reduced response time. We are not only limiting to the vulnerability assessment but also we are overall vulnerability management firm.
