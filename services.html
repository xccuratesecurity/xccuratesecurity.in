Our Services

Penetration Testing

It’s a tedious task of finding out potential loopholes in your software system by simulating an attack from outside. Since the test takes place in the perspective of a potential hacker even the minute vulnerability can be fished out to make the website/app hack proof. We at Xccurate Security make use of controlled, real-world attacks that can penetrate into your network and understand the resilience of the security solutions of your organization.

Web Application Security Testing

Applications are the favourite target of the attackers, also if the application doesn't undergo through penetration testing, then there are high chances that the application is might running with vulnerabilities. Using these vulnerabilities, it may lead to the massive user data breach.
Our expertise lies in identifying the weaknesses in your company’s applications. Our team has ample experience in the field to perform extensive web application security testing for you.
